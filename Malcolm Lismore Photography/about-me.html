About Me



Passionate Photographer of Scotland's Beauty

I am a freelance photographer based on the stunning North West coast of Scotland. My love for photography began with a deep appreciation for the region's rugged landscapes and diverse wildlife. Over the years, I have developed a strong portfolio that showcases my talent and passion for capturing nature's beauty.


Experience and Skills

With several years of experience in photography, I have a knack for capturing everything from the sweeping vistas of the Scottish Highlands to the intricate details of coastal wildlife. My work includes not only landscape and wildlife photography but also portraits, weddings, and special events.


A Love for Nature

My photography reflects my love for the natural world. I carefully observe my surroundings to create images that highlight the unique beauty of Scotland's landscapes and wildlife. My approach combines technical skill with a genuine passion for my subjects.


Versatile Services

In addition to my nature photography, I offer services for weddings, portraits, and events. I bring the same level of dedication and creativity to every project, ensuring that each client's needs are met with professionalism and care.
